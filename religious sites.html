Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib)

An iconic Sikh gurdwara in Amritsar, Punjab

The Golden Temple, also known as Harmandir Sahib, is the holiest Gurdwara of Sikhism located in the city of Amritsar, Punjab. It's known for its stunning architecture and serene environment.

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

One of the most famous Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

The Kashi Vishwanath Temple, also known as the Golden Temple of Varanasi, is one of the most revered Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is located in the ancient city of Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh.

Jagannath Temple

A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Jagannath in Puri, Odisha

The Jagannath Temple is a major pilgrimage site for Hindus, dedicated to Lord Jagannath (a form of Vishnu), located in Puri, Odisha. The temple is known for its annual Rath Yatra.

Badrinath Temple

One of the Char Dham and Chota Char Dham pilgrimage sites dedicated to Lord Vishnu in Uttarakhand

The Badrinath Temple is one of the most important pilgrimage sites for Hindus, dedicated to Lord Vishnu. It is located in the town of Badrinath in Uttarakhand, amidst the Himalayas.

Dilwara Temples

A group of Jain temples known for their intricate marble carvings in Mount Abu, Rajasthan

The Dilwara Temples are a group of beautifully carved Jain temples located in Mount Abu, Rajasthan. These temples are renowned for their stunning marble architecture and intricate carvings.

Akshardham Temple

A Hindu temple complex known for its architecture and spiritual exhibitions in Delhi

Akshardham is a Hindu temple complex in Delhi, India, known for its magnificent architecture and spiritual exhibitions. It is dedicated to Bhagwan Swaminarayan.
