Varanasi Ghats

An artistic and spiritual hub along the banks of the Ganges River

Varanasi, also known as Banaras or Kashi, is one of the oldest living cities in the world. Its ghats along the Ganges River are not only spiritual landmarks but also artistic centers hosting music, dance, and art performances.

Udaipur

A city of lakes and palaces, renowned for its traditional music and arts

Udaipur, located in Rajasthan, is known as the "City of Lakes" and is famous for its rich cultural heritage. It is a hub for traditional Rajasthani music, dance, and arts, with beautiful palaces and havelis as backdrops.

Learn more Official Site

Kerala Backwaters

A scenic network of lakes, canals, and lagoons where art forms thrive

The Kerala Backwaters are a unique network of interconnected canals, rivers, lakes, and inlets. This serene environment serves as an inspiration for various art forms including Kathakali dance, Kalaripayattu martial arts, and traditional music.

Learn more Official Site

Jaisalmer

An ancient city in the heart of the Thar Desert, known for its folk music and arts

Jaisalmer, located in Rajasthan, is a vibrant city renowned for its folk music and arts. The desert city hosts various cultural events, including the Rajasthan International Folk Festival (RIFF), showcasing traditional music and dance.
