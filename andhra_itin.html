Day 1: Arrival in Visakhapatnam

Preferred Route: Fly to Visakhapatnam International Airport.

Mode of Transport: Taxi from airport to hotel

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Description: Arrive in Visakhapatnam, known as the "City of Destiny". Check into your hotel and explore the beaches and attractions of this coastal city.

  • Check-in at hotel in Visakhapatnam
  • Visit Ramakrishna Beach
  • Explore Kailasagiri Hilltop Park
  • Enjoy local seafood delicacies

Day 2: Araku Valley Excursion

Preferred Route: Drive from Visakhapatnam to Araku Valley (Approx. 115 km).

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Taxi

Best Time to Visit: September to February

Description: Explore the scenic beauty of Araku Valley, nestled in the Eastern Ghats. Experience the coffee plantations, waterfalls, and tribal culture.

  • Drive through the Eastern Ghats
  • Visit Borra Caves
  • Explore Tribal Museum
  • Enjoy a ride on Araku Valley Train

Day 3: Vijayawada City Tour

Preferred Route: Drive from Visakhapatnam to Vijayawada (Approx. 350 km).

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Taxi

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Description: Travel to Vijayawada, a city known for its historical significance and religious sites. Explore the cultural and historical attractions of the city.

  • Visit Kanaka Durga Temple
  • Explore Undavalli Caves
  • Boating in Krishna River
  • Enjoy panoramic views from Prakasam Barrage

Day 4: Amaravati Exploration

Preferred Route: Drive from Vijayawada to Amaravati (Approx. 40 km).

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Taxi

Best Time to Visit: November to February

Description: Explore Amaravati, an ancient city with historical and archaeological significance. Discover the Buddhist heritage and ancient stupas.

  • Visit Amaravati Buddhist Site
  • Explore Amaravati Museum
  • Learn about ancient Buddhist relics
  • Enjoy local Andhra cuisine

Day 5: Departure from Visakhapatnam

Preferred Route: Drive from Amaravati to Visakhapatnam (Approx. 370 km).

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Taxi

Best Time to Visit: Anytime

Description: Depart from Visakhapatnam with beautiful memories of Andhra Pradesh's culture, history, and natural beauty.

  • Check-out from hotel in Visakhapatnam
  • Shopping at Jagadamba Junction
  • Transfer to Visakhapatnam Airport for departure
  • Safe journey back home
