Day 1: Ahmedabad to Vadodara

Preferred Route: Drive from Ahmedabad to Vadodara via NH48.

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Taxi

Best Time to Visit: October to February

Description: Start your journey from Ahmedabad, visit the historic Sabarmati Ashram, then drive to Vadodara to explore the majestic Laxmi Vilas Palace and the ancient Kirti Mandir.

  • Visit Sabarmati Ashram, Ahmedabad
  • Explore Laxmi Vilas Palace, Vadodara
  • Discover Kirti Mandir, Vadodara
  • Dinner at a local restaurant in Vadodara

Day 2: Vadodara to Rann of Kutch

Preferred Route: Drive from Vadodara to Rann of Kutch via NH947 and NH341.

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Taxi

Best Time to Visit: November to February

Description: Experience the vast white desert of Rann of Kutch during the Rann Utsav. Enjoy camel rides, traditional dance, and cultural performances.

  • Explore White Desert, Rann of Kutch
  • Participate in Rann Utsav activities
  • Enjoy local cuisine and cultural performances
  • Stay overnight in a tent at Rann of Kutch

Day 3: Rann of Kutch to Gir National Park

Preferred Route: Drive from Rann of Kutch to Gir National Park via GJ SH 6.

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Taxi

Best Time to Visit: December to March

Description: Embark on a safari adventure in Gir National Park, the only home of Asiatic Lions. Explore the diverse wildlife and serene surroundings.

  • Safari to Spot Asiatic Lions
  • Explore Gir Interpretation Zone
  • Visit Junagadh Fort and Uparkot Fort
  • Enjoy local Gujarati cuisine for dinner

Day 4: Gir to Somnath

Preferred Route: Drive from Gir to Somnath via GJ SH 26.

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Taxi

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Description: Visit the famous Somnath Temple, one of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva. Explore the beachside temple and enjoy the tranquil atmosphere.

  • Visit Somnath Temple
  • Explore Triveni Sangam, Somnath
  • Relax at Somnath Beach
  • Experience evening Aarti at the temple

Day 5: Somnath to Dwarka

Preferred Route: Drive from Somnath to Dwarka via GJ SH 33.

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Taxi

Best Time to Visit: November to February

Description: Explore the holy city of Dwarka, known for its ancient temples and religious significance. Visit the Dwarkadhish Temple and experience the divine aura of this sacred place.

  • Visit Dwarkadhish Temple, Dwarka
  • Explore Nageshwar Jyotirlinga Temple
  • Visit Rukmini Devi Temple
  • Enjoy a boat ride at Gomti Ghat
