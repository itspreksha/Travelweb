Jammu and Kashmir Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar

Morning

  • Arrive in Srinagar
  • Check-in at your houseboat or hotel

Afternoon

  • Enjoy a Shikara ride on Dal Lake
  • Visit the Mughal Gardens (Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi)

Evening

  • Explore the local markets and indulge in shopping
  • Try traditional Kashmiri cuisine for dinner

Day 2: Sightseeing in Srinagar

Morning

  • Visit Shankaracharya Temple
  • Explore the Old City and its historic sites

Afternoon

  • Take a trip to Pari Mahal and Hazratbal Shrine
  • Have lunch at a local restaurant

Evening

  • Enjoy a relaxing evening by the Dal Lake
  • Try some local street food for dinner

Day 3: Visit Gulmarg

Morning

  • Drive to Gulmarg, known as the "Meadow of Flowers"
  • Enjoy the scenic beauty and take a ride on the Gulmarg Gondola

Afternoon

  • Have lunch at one of the local restaurants
  • Explore the Gulmarg Biosphere Reserve

Evening

  • Return to Srinagar in the evening
  • Relax and enjoy the sunset at Dal Lake

Day 4: Visit Pahalgam

Morning

  • Drive to Pahalgam, known as the "Valley of Shepherds"
  • Explore Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, and Chandanwari

Afternoon

  • Enjoy a picnic lunch amidst the picturesque landscapes
  • Visit the local market and shop for souvenirs

Evening

  • Return to Srinagar in the evening
  • Experience a traditional Kashmiri dinner

Day 5: Departure from Srinagar

Morning

  • Check-out from your hotel or houseboat
  • Transfer to the airport for your departure

Afternoon

  • Departure from Srinagar
