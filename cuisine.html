Pick Your Trail

Amritsar

Home to the iconic Punjabi cuisine, including the famous Amritsari Kulcha

Amritsar, in the state of Punjab, is renowned for its rich and flavorful Punjabi cuisine. The city is famous for dishes like Amritsari Kulcha, Makki di Roti with Sarson ka Saag, and the iconic Langar at the Golden Temple.

Rajasthan

Experience the royal Rajasthani cuisine with dishes like Dal Baati Churma and Gatte ki Sabzi

Rajasthani cuisine is a reflection of the state's royal heritage. Indulge in dishes like Dal Baati Churma, Gatte ki Sabzi, and Laal Maas. The cuisine is rich in flavors and often includes dishes made with gram flour and spices.

Kolkata Street Food

Discover the vibrant street food culture of Kolkata with treats like Puchka, Kathi Rolls, and Rosogolla

Kolkata is famous for its diverse street food offerings. Indulge in delicacies like Puchka (Pani Puri), Kathi Rolls, Jhalmuri, and the iconic Rosogolla. The city's streets are a paradise for food enthusiasts.

Hyderabadi Cuisine

Experience the flavors of Hyderabadi Biryani, Haleem, and Double Ka Meetha

Hyderabadi cuisine is a blend of Mughlai, Turkish, and Arabic influences. Try iconic dishes like Hyderabadi Biryani, Haleem, and Double Ka Meetha. The city of Hyderabad is a paradise for food lovers.

Kerala Cuisine

Explore the diverse flavors of Kerala with dishes like Appam, Fish Moilee, and Banana Chips

Kerala cuisine is known for its use of coconut, spices, and seafood. Enjoy dishes like Appam with Stew, Fish Moilee, Puttu, and Kerala Sadya. The state's cuisine offers a unique blend of flavors.
