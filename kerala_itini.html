Kerala is well-known for its mesmerizing backwaters, ecotourism initiatives, scenic beauty, amazing beaches, and mouthwatering cuisine.

Day 1: Arrival in Kochi

Preferred Route: Fly to Kochi International Airport.

Mode of Transport: Taxi from airport to hotel

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Description: Arrive in Kochi, also known as the "Queen of the Arabian Sea". Check into your hotel and explore the historical sites and backwaters of Fort Kochi.

  • Check-in at hotel in Fort Kochi
  • Visit Mattancherry Palace
  • Explore Fort Kochi Beach
  • Witness Chinese Fishing Nets

Day 2: Munnar Hill Station

Preferred Route: Drive from Kochi to Munnar (Approx. 130 km).

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Taxi

Best Time to Visit: September to May

Description: Travel to Munnar, a beautiful hill station known for its tea gardens and panoramic views of Western Ghats.

  • Drive through scenic routes
  • Visit Tea Gardens and Tea Museum
  • Explore Mattupetty Dam
  • Experience Echo Point and Photo Point

Day 3: Alleppey Backwaters

Preferred Route: Drive from Munnar to Alleppey (Approx. 175 km).

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Taxi

Best Time to Visit: August to February

Description: Experience the famous backwaters of Kerala in Alleppey. Enjoy a houseboat stay and witness the serene beauty of backwater villages.

  • Check-in to Houseboat in Alleppey
  • Cruise through backwater canals
  • Enjoy traditional Kerala meals on houseboat
  • Witness sunset over the backwaters

Day 4: Thekkady Wildlife Sanctuary

Preferred Route: Drive from Alleppey to Thekkady (Approx. 140 km).

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Taxi

Best Time to Visit: September to March

Description: Explore the wildlife and spice plantations of Thekkady. Engage in a jungle safari and experience the rich biodiversity.

  • Visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Enjoy a boat ride on Periyar Lake
  • Explore Spice Plantations
  • Witness Kathakali Performance in the evening

Day 5: Departure from Kochi

Preferred Route: Drive from Thekkady to Kochi (Approx. 155 km).

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Taxi

Best Time to Visit: Anytime

Description: Depart from Kochi with unforgettable memories of Kerala's natural beauty and cultural heritage.

  • Check-out from hotel in Kochi
  • Visit Jewish Synagogue and Dutch Palace
  • Shopping in local markets
  • Transfer to Kochi Airport for departure
