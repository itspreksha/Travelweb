Rajasthan Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur

Morning

  • Arrive in Jaipur
  • Check-in at your hotel

Afternoon

  • Have lunch at a local restaurant
  • Visit the City Palace
  • Explore Jantar Mantar

Evening

  • Enjoy shopping in the local markets
  • Dinner at a traditional Rajasthani restaurant

Day 2: Sightseeing in Jaipur

Morning

  • Visit the Amber Fort
  • Enjoy an elephant ride (optional)

Afternoon

  • Explore Hawa Mahal
  • Visit Jal Mahal

Evening

  • Attend a cultural show
  • Enjoy dinner at a rooftop restaurant with a view of the city

Day 3: Travel to Jodhpur

Morning

  • Drive to Jodhpur
  • Stop at Pushkar en route

Afternoon

  • Explore the Brahma Temple in Pushkar
  • Have lunch at a local restaurant

Evening

  • Arrive in Jodhpur
  • Check-in at your hotel

Day 4: Sightseeing in Jodhpur

Morning

  • Visit Mehrangarh Fort
  • Explore Jaswant Thada

Afternoon

  • Have lunch at a local restaurant
  • Visit Umaid Bhawan Palace

Evening

  • Enjoy a relaxing evening at your hotel
  • Optional: Explore the local markets

Day 5: Departure from Jodhpur

Morning

  • Check-out from your hotel
  • Transfer to the airport or railway station

Afternoon

  • Departure from Jodhpur
