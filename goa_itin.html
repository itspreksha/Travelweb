Goa can be the perfect destination for a short holiday. Its sandy beaches and sunny weather attract people from across the globe.

Day 1: Arrival in Goa

Preferred Route: Fly to Goa International Airport (Dabolim Airport).

Mode of Transport: Taxi from airport to hotel

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Description: Arrive in Goa and check into your hotel or resort. Relax and enjoy the beaches or explore the local markets and nightlife.

  • Check-in at hotel/resort
  • Relax at the nearest beach
  • Visit local markets for shopping
  • Experience Goa's nightlife

Day 2: North Goa Exploration

Preferred Route: Drive from your hotel in North Goa.

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Scooter

Best Time to Visit: November to February

Description: Explore the vibrant and lively beaches of North Goa. Visit famous landmarks and indulge in water sports activities.

  • Visit Calangute Beach
  • Explore Fort Aguada
  • Relax at Anjuna Beach
  • Enjoy water sports at Baga Beach

Day 3: South Goa Delights

Preferred Route: Drive from your hotel in South Goa.

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Taxi

Best Time to Visit: October to February

Description: Explore the serene and pristine beaches of South Goa. Discover the rich heritage and tranquility of this region.

  • Visit Palolem Beach
  • Explore Dudhsagar Waterfalls (Jeep Safari)
  • Discover Old Goa - Churches and Basilicas
  • Relax at Agonda Beach

Day 4: Adventure and Nature

Preferred Route: Drive to your adventure spots.

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Adventure Tour

Best Time to Visit: November to February

Description: Engage in thrilling adventures and explore the natural beauty of Goa's hinterland.

  • Go for a Jungle Trek in Mollem National Park
  • Visit Spice Plantations for a tour
  • Experience Kayaking in the backwaters
  • Enjoy a Sunset Cruise on Mandovi River

Day 5: Departure from Goa

Preferred Route: Drive to Goa International Airport (Dabolim Airport).

Mode of Transport: Taxi from hotel to airport

Best Time to Visit: Anytime

Description: Pack your memories and depart from Goa with unforgettable experiences.

  • Check-out from hotel
  • Shopping for souvenirs
  • Transfer to Goa Airport
  • Safe Journey back home
