Maharashtra Itinerary

Day 1: Mumbai to Lonavala

Preferred Route: Drive from Mumbai to Lonavala via Mumbai-Pune Expressway.

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Train

Best Time to Visit: June to September (Monsoon)

Description: Begin your journey from Mumbai and head towards the picturesque hill station of Lonavala. Enjoy the lush greenery, waterfalls, and serene landscapes.

  • Visit Bhushi Dam
  • Explore Tiger's Point
  • Enjoy local street food
  • Stay overnight in Lonavala

Day 2: Lonavala to Mahabaleshwar

Preferred Route: Drive from Lonavala to Mahabaleshwar via NH48.

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Taxi

Best Time to Visit: October to February

Description: Journey from Lonavala to the charming hill station of Mahabaleshwar, known for its strawberry farms and stunning viewpoints.

  • Explore Venna Lake
  • Visit Elephant's Head Point
  • Enjoy a horse ride to Pratapgad Fort
  • Try fresh strawberries and local delicacies

Day 3: Mahabaleshwar to Pune

Preferred Route: Drive from Mahabaleshwar to Pune via NH48.

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Taxi

Best Time to Visit: September to March

Description: Travel to Pune, the cultural capital of Maharashtra. Explore the city's historical sites, vibrant markets, and delicious food.

  • Visit Shaniwar Wada
  • Explore Aga Khan Palace
  • Shop at Pune's famous markets
  • Enjoy local Maharashtrian cuisine for dinner

Day 4: Pune to Nashik

Preferred Route: Drive from Pune to Nashik via NH60.

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Bus

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Description: Journey to Nashik, known for its vineyards and temples. Explore the wine culture and religious sites in this beautiful city.

  • Visit Sula Vineyards
  • Explore Trimbakeshwar Temple
  • Enjoy a boat ride in Gangapur Dam
  • Try local wines and cuisine

Day 5: Nashik to Mumbai

Preferred Route: Drive from Nashik to Mumbai via NH160 and NH3.

Mode of Transport: Self-drive or Train

Best Time to Visit: November to February

Description: Return journey to Mumbai, the bustling metropolis. Explore the city's iconic landmarks, vibrant markets, and diverse culture.

  • Visit Gateway of India
  • Explore Marine Drive
  • Shop at Colaba Causeway
  • Enjoy a sunset at Bandra Bandstand
